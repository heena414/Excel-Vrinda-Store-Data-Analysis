Vrinda Store 2022 Annual Report: Data Analysis and Insights


Objective 

Vrinda Store aims to analyze its performance in 2022 to better understand customer behavior and drive increased sales in 2023.


Problem Statements Addressed:

- Sales and Orders Comparison (2022): Analyzed monthly sales and order trends.
- Peak Sales Month: Identified the month with the highest sales and orders.
- Customer Demographics: Compared product purchases between men and women.
- Order Status Analysis: Reviewed the various order statuses throughout 2022.
- Top Sales-Contributing States: Ranked the top 5 states by sales contribution.
- Age-Gender Relationship: Examined the relationship between age, gender, and the number of orders.
- Sales Channels: Determined which sales channels contributed the most to revenue.


Key Insights:

- Customer Demographics: Women are more likely to purchase products than men, making up 65% of total sales.
- Top-Contributing States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top three states, contributing to 35% of total sales.
- Age Group Contribution: The adult age group (30-49 years) is the largest contributor, responsible for nearly 50% of orders.
- Sales Channels: Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the top-performing channels, accounting for 80% of total sales.
- Order Fulfillment: Over 92% of the total orders were successfully delivered.
- Monthly Performance: March recorded the highest sales, while November had the lowest.


Skills Utilized:

- Data Cleaning: Ensured accuracy and consistency in the dataset.
- Data Processing: Organized and transformed raw data for analysis.
- Pivot Tables: Used for summarizing large datasets and drawing insights.
- Slicers: Enabled interactive filtering to focus on specific data segments.
- Data Visualization: Created compelling visual dashboards to communicate findings effectively.


Conclusion: 

To boost sales in 2023, Vrinda Store should focus on targeting women aged 30-40 years in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh. Tailoring marketing strategies with ads, offers, and coupons on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra will likely yield the best results.
